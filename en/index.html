Philosophy At Agro Granadera Santa Maria del Payaguay, we cultivate a sustainable production model that respects and values the environment. Our regenerative agriculture improves soil quality, promoting biodiversity and preservation of natural resources. Our livestock is 100% free-range, with animals raised outdoors, ensuring welfare and ecological balance.

Agriculture In our agricultural project, our greatest responsibility is to maintain and increase soil quality, as it is the medium where everything grows and develops. Thus, technologies focused on these improvements are part of our daily routine. The quality of grain drying and cleaning is also part of our concern, since they will feed people around the world. The basic idea is to keep the soil 100% covered throughout the year, making the correct sequence of species in direct seeding the foundation of our entire system.

Livestock In this sector, we develop a rustic animal in the field. It is a mix of Angus, Blond Aquitane, and Nelore breeds, fed with pasture and supplemented with grains, without hormones. There is no indiscriminate use of veterinary products; We use homeopathy as an important tool in parasite control. It is a noble product made to reach higher standards for consumers who want quality, but with commitment to social and environmental responsibility.

Yu'sara Additionally, we maintain a certified forest reserve where we harvest Yu'sara ecologically, without compromising forest regeneration. We complement this initiative with the manufacture of sustainable furniture, using only wood from fallen trees or responsible management.

Forest Creation Santa Maria Del Payaguay is dedicated to producing high-quality and sustainable wooden furniture, which not only enhances the beauty of homes and businesses but also preserves the environment. Our goal is to combine craftsmanship, innovation, and environmental responsibility to create lasting value for our customers, employees, and the community.
